About Panorama Destination

Part of Panorama Leisure Group, Panorama Destination was founded in December 1999 and is made up of more than 500 tourism professionals and 250 licensed tour guides, specializing in Indonesia inbound travel services. We operate a network of offices across Indonesia, providing unmatched tourism services throughout the archipelago. With branches in key tourist destinations of Sumatra, Java, Bali, Lombok, Flores and Sulawesi, Panorama Destination provides a standardized service nationwide.

Panorama Destination is Indonesia’s leading tour operator, awarded ‘Best Tour Operator in Indonesia’ 2007, 2008, 2013, 2014 and 2015 by TTG Asia and Most Popular Destination Company for MICE in 2015. Last year alone, more than 115,000 inbound tourists trusted their travel arrangements to us.

To provide the best information and services to our clients, Panorama Destination designs and delivers unique tour programs based on extensive local research and complemented by a range of flexible bespoke options. Our products include MICE and luxury travel, diving and snorkelling, wellness and spa packages, beach trips, cruises, golfing holidays, family vacations, wedding and honeymoon packages, trekking, cycling and culinary experiences. Our customers are truly international, coming from every corner of the globe and trusting their travel arrangements to us. Furthermore, we have forged a portfolio of fruitful partnerships alongside tour operators and travel specialists from all over the world.

As a member of the Global Sustainable Tourism Council (GSTC), we collaborate with UN agencies, the Indonesian government, international experts and local communities to establish and implement international standards. Our shared goal is to maximize social, cultural, economic and environmental benefits while minimizing the negative impacts of tourism and development. Panorama Destination is at the forefront of sustainable tourism in Indonesia and plays an important role in global initiatives to support ethical tourism.

Panorama Destination offers over 150 different tours catering to the needs of families, professionals, groups, individuals and couples. We build itineraries that are tailor made to suit the wishes of any traveller. Discovery tours take in a broad range of hotspots, or visitors can choose to delve deeper into local cultures in a number of superb destinations. Packages focus on sustainability and are created in harmony with local cultures, customs and communities. With offices nationwide, licensed and courteous local guides, outstanding local knowledge and a fleet of our own comfortable and reliable vehicles, Panorama Destination joins the dots on a traveller’s map and opens up a range of interesting locations, bringing the world to Indonesia.
